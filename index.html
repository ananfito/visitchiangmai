Visit Chiang Mai

Enjoy rich culture and food in this vibrant city of Northern Thailand

Top three activities to do in Chiang Mai

Visit a Thai Buddhist temple

Chiang Mai has dozens of Thai Buddhist temples—big and small—sprinkled throughout the city. Each one with a unique story.

Go to a market for tasty treats and souvenirs

There are three main markets to visit: Warorot, Night Bazaar, and Jing Jai. Each with its unique offerings depending on your craving.

Climb a mountain and see a waterfall

The city is surrounded by mountains with countless waterfalls nestled within them. Some are ten minutes away from the city center while others are more elusive.
